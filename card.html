Starfish

A Starfish in not a fish.They have no brain and no blood

  • Scientific Name: Asteroidea
  • Length: five to ten inches
  • Average Lifespan: 35 years
  • Habitat: tropical coral reefs, rocky shores, tidal pools, mud, and sand to kelp forests, seagrass meadows and the deep-sea floor down to at least 6,000 m (20,000 ft)

Starfish or sea stars are star-shaped echinoderms belonging to the class Asteroidea. Common usage frequently finds these names being also applied to ophiuroids, which are correctly referred to as brittle stars or "basket stars".
